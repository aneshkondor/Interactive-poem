Beyond Borders

Hover over the highlighted parts of the poem to explore more about Sangeetha's life. On small screens, tap for details.

Under homeland skies, dreams awoke,
Bound by home, with emotions untold.
“A final embrace, love burning bright,
A step into darkness, chasing light.”

In America’s tech world, she began her quest,
Balancing work and studies, she gave her best.
“Yet something stirred, deep within,
A calling to rise, and begin again.”

“A dream for her family,” a future in sight,
A school she built, with love as her light.
Through every challenge, she paved her own way,
“Building a legacy that shines each day.”

Now she reflects, with wisdom and grace,
A life well-lived, a meaningful space.
Her message clear, her voice profound:
“In every challenge, hope is found.”
